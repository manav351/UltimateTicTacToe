Instructions

In a game of ultimate tic tac toe you have 9 regular games that combined makes a single big game.
  1. X always moves first.
  2. The first player can Mark anywhere out of the 9 Grids.
  3. The next player has to play in the outer grid which the previous player determines by his moves in inner grid.
  4. Similarly, the game continues
  5. If the previous player targets the next player to a grid which is already taken, then the next player will get a free move
  6. The players have to make Tic Tac Toe pattern in the outer grid to win the game
